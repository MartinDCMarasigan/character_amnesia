提筆忘字 Character Amnesia

Since the beginning of the digital age, the ability to write kanji has decreased among Japanese people. As people continue to rely on computers and smartphones, they are forgetting how to write kanji that were common to write in the past.

Opportunities to write, 1995–2021

According to the Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications of Japan, the ownership of digital devices have continued to rise since 1995. At the same time, the Agency of Cultural Affairs of Japan have reported a decline for writing opportunities throughout the nation.

The Tablet Replaces Paper in Schools

As the use of tablets for education increases in schools, children are getting less opportunities to write despite having rigorous lessons in writing kanji.

Character Amnesia Experienced by All Ages

With the use of digital devices by all ages in Japan in all aspects of their lives, from domestic life to work, even people above 40 who are well into their senior years see diminished performance in writing kanji.
